Cancellation Policy

Understand our cancellation and refund rules

1. Free Cancellation

Cancellations made up to 48 hours before the check-in date will receive a full refund to the original payment method.

2. Late Cancellation

Cancellations made within 48 hours of the scheduled check-in will result in a charge equivalent to one night's stay. Any remaining nights will be refunded.

3. No Show

If you fail to check in without prior cancellation, you will be charged for the entire booking. No refund will be issued.

4. Group Bookings

Group reservations have different cancellation terms. Please contact our sales team for specific policies applicable to your group booking.

5. Special Events & Offers

Cancellation policies for special packages and promotional rates may differ. Always review the terms at the time of booking.

6. Refund Processing

All refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days of cancellation confirmation.

7. Changes to Booking

Changes to check-in/check-out dates are permitted without penalty if done 48 hours before arrival. After this period, the late cancellation policy applies.

8. Conditions & Exceptions

Certain conditions such as natural disasters, pandemics, or force majeure may affect cancellation policies. Our team will communicate any necessary updates.
